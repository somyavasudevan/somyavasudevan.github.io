Welcome to Claw4Twitter

Get a better twitter experience. Automatically find and apply hashtags while you're typing, know if a tweet is malicious, get sentiments of the typed tweets and find out where they're popular!

#

Hashtag suggestion

Relevant hashtags are suggested for the tweets as and when the user is typing ! Also, the sentiment of the tweet is displayed, along with the past sentiments of the main entities.

Malicious Tweets

We use crowd sourcing to detect tweets with malicious contents (offensive tweets, fake news, etc) and mark them.

Tweet hotspots

The locations where tweets are popular is shown. This aids digital marketing and helps users target a particular location.
